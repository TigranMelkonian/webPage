In May 2018, I graduated from Boston University with a B.A. degree in Pure and Applied Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science. Passion

In a time where understanding and innovating with data has the undeniable potential to improve the way society functions, I hope to join the community of data scientists that will use data to drive social change.
